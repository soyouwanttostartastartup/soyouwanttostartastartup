The Snapshot

Columbia's resources for entreprenuership come from both the school's administration and various clubs found on campus. From student led venture capital groups to the Columbia Venture Competition, Columbia's resources enable students to hit the ground running. Because of this support, students have particular advantages that aren't as accessible otherwise.
