Student Founders at Columbia

Columbia entreprenuers operate in different industries. Interested in getting advice on your company? Consider reaching out to one of the following students!

$25,000 Winner of the Columbia Venture
Competition #StartupColumbia track 2016

Sara Sakowitz, Columbia College Class of 2018

Founder/CEO of Blue Moon Box and Partner at Dorm Room Fund

Sara founded Blue Moon Box in 2014, a subscription box product with interactive science projects delivered straight to your door - every month! Sara was recently named to Crains New York's 20 Under 20 List for BMB's success.

Sara is an operating partner at Dorm Room Fund and was the President of the Columbia Organization for Rising Entreprenuers from 2016-2017.

Email Address: sarasakowitz@columbia.edu

$25,000 Winner of the Columbia Venture
Competition Undergraduate track 2016

Mathew Pregasen, Columbia Engineering Class of 2018

Founder/CEO of Parsegon

Mathew, with Anuke Ganegoda (Columbia '18) and Sahir Jaggi (Columbia '17), founded Parsegon, an education technology company building tools for STEM teachers. With the first English to math algorithm, it makes digital math extremely convinient and flexible for educators.

Parsegon is funded by Dorm Room Fund and Rough Draft Ventures.

Email Address: mathew@parsegon.com
